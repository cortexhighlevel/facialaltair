

## FitFlow Hero Section

### Layout
- Full-viewport hero section (100vh) with a fitness background image covering the entire area
- Pink-to-blue gradient overlay (left pink → right teal/blue) blending with the image
- Content positioned at the bottom-left and bottom-right with generous padding (64px)

### Elements

**Top-left:** "FitFlow" logo text (white, bold)

**Top-right:** "MENU" pill button (pink/coral background, white text, rounded)

**Mid-left:** TrustPoint rating badge — "Excellent 4.9 out of 5 ★ TrustPoint" in small white text

**Bottom-left — Large headline:**
- "Stronger." / "Healthier." / "You." — stacked, massive white bold text (~120px+), taking up ~50% width

**Bottom-right — Description + CTA:**
- Subtitle: "Transform your body and mindset with expert online coaching. Personalized training, real results, and full support — wherever you are." (white text, ~16px)
- "Start Your Journey" button — white background, dark text, rounded pill shape with an arrow icon

### Background
- Full-bleed fitness image (woman exercising) with object-fit: cover
- Gradient overlay from pink (left) to teal/blue (right) using `linear-gradient(90deg, rgba(245,148,155,0.85), rgba(188,124,183,0.7), rgba(68,171,180,0.5))`
